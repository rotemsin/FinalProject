This distribution uses a rating of all 
teams, sorting them from the 
strongest to the weakest according to 
their odds of winning the World Cup. 
The odds of team A, that has an odd 
of 1/i to win the World Cup to beat
team B, that has an odd of 1/j to win
the World Cup is 
(1 / j) / ((1 / i) + (1 / j)).
